Sith or Jedi?

Sith

Siths are force sensitive and fall towards the dark side. The lightsaber color that is most popular among the siths is red.

Jedi

Jedis are force sensitive and fall towards the light side. The main lightsaber colors of the Jedi were green and blue, but now also include yellow and purple.

Neither Sith nor Jedi

These force sensitive beings aren't a part of the Sith or Jedi side. These characters make their own path and the main lightsaber color for them is white.
